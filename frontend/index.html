How it works

4 steps to interview confidence

Setup takes 30 seconds. Then just use it during your call.

1

Open this page in Chrome or Edge

Scroll down to the tool. Add your job role and context for personalized answers. Keep it in a separate window from your video call.

2

Click "Start listening" and allow mic

The tool continuously listens. Everything said in the interview is transcribed in real time — questions, follow-ups, everything.

3

Press Ctrl+G when you need an answer

The AI reads the transcribed question and generates a perfect, confident answer in about 2 seconds. Glance, absorb, respond naturally.

4

Press Ctrl+H if asked to share screen

Instantly hides everything — screen goes completely black. Press Ctrl+H again once screen share ends to bring it back.
